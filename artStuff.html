I like to draw, specifically digital art.

My Art Origins

My interest in drawing began around 2012 when I drew Phineas and Ferb fan art in my sketchbook. Eventually, I drew fan art of different anime characters and moved on to making art of my own original characters.

I started taking digital drawing seriously around 2018. At first, I was using Autodesk Sketchbook. Around 2020, I tried out Krita, which is a free art program. After trying out Krita for digital drawing, I've been using it as my main art program.
