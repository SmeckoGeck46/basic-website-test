One of my hobbies is making music, whether I compose music or do music production.

My Music-Making Origins

I was inspired to try making music after seeing my brother make original compositions. In 2019, I tried out composing music in MAGIX Music Maker. Eventually, I transitioned to using free programs like Cakewalk by Bandlab, GarageBand, LMMS, and Waveform Free.

The Music I Make
